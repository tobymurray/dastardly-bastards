Open to newcomers

Monthly One-Shots

Once a month we run a self-contained one-shot session — no prior experience needed, no ongoing commitment. It's how most new players get their foot in the door and see if the group is a good fit.

📖
Not currently recruiting

Full Campaigns

Our active campaigns are mid-story and not set up for new players to drop in. If a spot opens up down the road, we'll reach out to people who've already played a one-shot with us.
